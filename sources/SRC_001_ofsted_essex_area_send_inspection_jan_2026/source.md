# SRC_001: Area SEND inspection of Essex Local Area Partnership

## Summary

This source is the Ofsted and Care Quality Commission local area partnership report for Essex. The inspection took place from 19 January 2026 to 23 January 2026.

The inspection outcome states that the local area partnership's arrangements lead to inconsistent experiences and outcomes for children and young people with SEND, and that the local area partnership must work jointly to make improvements.

This source is relevant to SEND pathway planning, local offer communication, EHC plan timeliness and quality, neurodevelopmental assessment waits, health-service access, preparation for adulthood, and variation in support across localities.

## Scope

This source covers the Essex local area partnership, including Essex County Council and three Integrated Care Boards.

The source covers experiences and outcomes for children and young people with SEND in Essex, with findings about:

- EHC needs assessments and plans
- communication with families and professionals
- local offer awareness
- early and accurate identification of need
- neurodevelopmental assessment waits
- access to health services
- speech and language support
- emotional wellbeing and mental health support
- preparation for adulthood
- alternative provision
- co-production and family engagement
